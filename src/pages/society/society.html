Star Wars Family

Who we are ?

The Star Wars Family is fans' club dedicated to the creation and to the port of faithful retorts of armors of the imperial storm troops and the imperial uniforms of star Wars's universe. Created by Albin Johnson and Tom Crews in 1997, the group showed 4 000 members in 2008, making of him the biggest group dressed up of fans of star Wars's universe (record officialized by Guiness Book 20081). The members of the 501th occur often during conventions of science fiction and demonstrations of charity, sometimes at the request of the service of the relations with the fans of Lucasfilm.
